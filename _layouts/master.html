Calories: 300 kcl Serving: 1
Time: 45 min Difficulty: medium

INGREDIENTS

400g rice cake
1 fish cake
2 cups of water
1/2 green onio
3 spoons of hot pepper paste
1 spoon of sugar
1 spoon of soy sauce
1 spoon of hot pepper powder
1 spoon of corn syrup
1 spoon of chopped garlic

STEPS
1.First,cut the fish cake into half like a triangle and make slices of green onion.

2.Put in two cups of water into the wok and also put the ingredients too.
3.After the water starts to boil, put in rice cake into the wok.

4.Boil it
